Steam is Value's digital distribution plaform. It offers vast catalog of digital video games and software titles that are available to purchase and download, including very popular AAA and indie titles and various of multimedia content on their platform.

  

It also has a robust community on their social networking side on the platform, allowing for users to connect with their friends, join various groups, participate in online discussions on forums and share their gameplay experiences with the millions of players across the globe.
