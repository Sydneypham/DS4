UBISOFT CONNECT

A digital distribution and communications platform, Ubisoft Connect is your place to access any games exclusively published and owned by Ubisoft Entertainment.
Ubisoft Connect offers it's users an online profile system along with an online trophy reward system, which can be shared through Ubisoft Club.
By completeing in-game achievements users use their earned reward points to purchase in-game content.

  

Ubisoft Connect also offers a social media feature where users can create friends lists to invite friends to games and even compare achievements. In addition, Ubisoft Connect provides an optional subscription called Ubisoft+ that enables access to exclusive and new games as well as closed game beta's.



To learn more about Ubisoft Connect visit their website!
